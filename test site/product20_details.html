-wipes

Airplane wipes, also known as aircraft wipes or travel wipes, are specially designed moist towelettes intended for use during flights. These wipes are typically infused with cleansing agents, such as alcohol or disinfectants, to help passengers maintain cleanliness and hygiene while traveling. They come individually packaged for convenience and are often used to clean hands, tray tables, armrests, and other surfaces in the airplane cabin. Airplane wipes are particularly useful for passengers who want to freshen up during long flights or who wish to disinfect commonly touched surfaces to reduce the risk of germ transmission.
